FoodCam2.0

A radical proposal to prioritize
plant-based food at the Media Lab

  • Learn more

Did you know meat among the largest contributors to the Media Lab's greenhouse emissions, even more than electricty or HVAC?*

Foodcam2.0 is a climate initiative at the Media Lab to rethink our relationship to food. Let's pave the way for a more sustainable future together by prioritizing delicious vegetables.

Such a provocative proposal requires understanding the complexities of both carbon emissions, and the beauracracy of our own department. This website is designed to start a conversation about how the lab can prototype new possibilities for sustainability. Read more about the research we have done, how this proposal would unfold, and take the survey to have your voice heard.
