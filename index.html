Food Futures

A radical climate action proposal to transform
all food at at the Media Lab to plant-based

  • 🌱 Learn more 🌎

Did you know food is among the largest contributors to the Media Lab's greenhouse emissions, even more than electricity or HVAC?*

We could cut these emissions by 50% by committing to plant-based food! Food Futures is a climate initiative at the Media Lab which proposes to transition all food expenditures to plant-based. We propose a FoodCam2.0: turning the camera back onto ourselves to translate our values into climate action.

🥦 🥑 🍏 🍊 🍅 🌽 🫑 🥒 🫒 🍇 🥦 🥑 🍏 🍊 🍅 🌽 🫑 🥒 🫒 🍇

Such a provocative proposal requires understanding the complexities of both carbon emissions, and the beauracracy of our own department. This website is designed to start a conversation about how the lab can prototype new possibilities for sustainability. Read more about the research we have done, how this proposal would unfold, and take the survey to have your voice heard.
