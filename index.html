Did you know food is among the largest contributors to the Media Lab's greenhouse emissions, even more than electricity or HVAC?*

We could cut these emissions by 50% by committing to plant-based food! Food Futures is a climate initiative at the Media Lab which proposes to transition all food expenditures to plant-based. We propose a FoodCam2.0: turning the camera back onto ourselves to translate our values into climate action.
