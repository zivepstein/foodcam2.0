Where can we get delicious plant-based foods?

So this proposal doesn't create extra annoying work for those buying food, here we have crowdsourced a list of local food options with plant-based options so tasty you won't be missing meat at all.




How to interact with your research group

One good way to talk about this is it is not a ban on all animal-based food in the Media Lab. We do not deal in black and whites, and don't expect this to happen overnight. One way to compromise at the beginning by fostering a different distribution of food options, and this can come in many forms, such as:

  1. Subbing out beef (the biggest contributor of GHG by a long shot) for chicken
  2. A menu of 85% plant-based and 15% meat.
  3. Going pescetarian!
  4. For weekly food purchases, alternating "normal" food consuption with a veggie options.

Food is about community, and this is a conversation that must be had within your community. We know different approaches will work for different situations, and we only hope this starts those conversations
